Mission Statement

The theories of Quantum Mechanics and General Relativity are foundational to modern physics. Despite remarkable advancements, these theories have unresolved conflicts, posing significant scientific challenges.

The Inductica Physics Project

  • Write a theory of induction as a systematic process for deriving scientific laws.
  • Use this theory to overcome current physical conflicts and broaden understanding.
  • Accelerate modern physics exploration through systematic thinking.
