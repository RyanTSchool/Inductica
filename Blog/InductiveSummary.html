An Inductive Summary of Physics

By James March 30, 2021 6:00am
In this lecture series I give an overview of the observations and reasoning steps in a possible inductive order which prove the essential principles of physics. This overview is a prelude to a more rigorous inductive validation of the principles of physics, which will employ my theory of induction.
