Mission Statement

By James November 28, 2023 6:07 am
The theories of Quantum Mechanics and General Relativity are flawed and are hindering progress toward new fundamental discoveries in physics. In order to replace these theories, we must go back to the observations and reinterpret them. However, in an extremely advanced science like physics, observations can only be interpreted using earlier established theories. As a result, though many scientists agree that Quantum Mechanics and General Relativity need to be corrected, none of them have been able to return to the observations and look at them with a perspective which is not colored by aspects of existing flawed theories. The Inductive Physics Project will overcome these challenges in three phases.

1. Write a theory of induction: a systematic process for proving scientific conclusions from observation. 2. Use that theory to reprove the essential principles of known physics from observation. This will allow us to approach quantum and relativistic phenomena only using prior theories which can be known with certainty. 3. Armed with a clear background knowledge which is free of faulty assumptions, the Inductive Physics Project will then develop new theories explaining quantum and relativistic phenomena. The Inductive Physics Project will then use this unprecedented knowledge state toward two productive ends: 1. Make new discoveries in physics. 2. Use new theories and discoveries to produce new technologies for profit.
